Title: Remarks in an Exchange With Reporters Prior to Departure for Baltimore, Maryland
Date: December 13, 2025
Source: https://www.presidency.ucsb.edu/documents/remarks-exchange-with-reporters-prior-departure-for-baltimore-maryland-1

Death of Three U.S. Servicemembers in Palmyra, Syria

Q. Mr. President, do you have a response to the attack in Syria?

The President. So we mourn the loss of three great patriots in Syria. You know how it happened. It was an ambush. Terrible. We also have three wounded that seem to be doing pretty well. But we mourn the loss. These are great—three great people. And it's just a terrible thing.

Syria, by the way, was fighting along with us. The President—the new President of Syria is, as they told me, and I'm not surprised, he's devastated by what happened. This was an ISIS attack on us and Syria.

And, again, we mourn the loss, and we pray for them and their parents and their loved ones. Very, very sad.

Islamic State of Iraq and Syria (ISIS) Terrorist Organization

Q. Mr. President, will the U.S. retaliate against ISIS?

The President. Yes. We will.

U.S. Counterterrorism Operations

Q. And will—and if U.S. forces are attacked again?

The President. You're going to be seeing. You'll be seeing. We will retaliate.

Thank you very much.
