Thank you. Please raise your right hands.

I—and state your name—do solemnly swear that I will support and defend the Constitution of the United States against all enemies, foreign and domestic; that I will bear true faith and allegiance to the same; and that I will obey the orders of the President of the United States and the orders of the officers appointed over me, according to regulations and the Uniform Code of Military Justice. So help me God.

Congratulations. Congratulations.

NOTE: The President spoke at 6:01 p.m. at Northwestern Stadium. The transcript was released by the Office of Communications on November 14.
